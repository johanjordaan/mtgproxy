instructions

  • paste your deck in the window on the left
  • click the Generate... button
  • wait for the pdf to be generated
  • download or view your pdf

features

  • the printout contains the decklist and the sideboard on the first page
  • only the relevant details are printed on the proxy card
  • sideboard cards are clearly marked on the proxy
  • 9 cards generated per A4 page

notes

  • compatable with most standard deck formtats (look here for examples)
  • the card name has to exectly correct (including case)
  • the download will expire in about 30 minutes. don't worry, you can always regenerate the pdf
